Peom by Nnamdi Alagwu

Nigeria like the cat
Nigeria like the cat
With nine lives
She’s so slithery
And so rich, among
The countries of blacks
Who revers and calls her
“Our big brother,”the giant
Of all Africans.
Nigeria like the cat
With nine lives
And, even though she’s rich
And so great
perfurbance abound!
Hullaballoo now hullaballoo then
Turmoil here, turmoil there.
